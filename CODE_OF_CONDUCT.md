# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
We want a welcoming, professional, harassment-free environment for anyone participating in this project.

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews) and to public spaces when someone is representing this project.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent, give constructive feedback
- Keep discussions focused on the work
- Respect privacy and consent

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks, trolling, sustained disruption
- Sexualized language/imagery directed at others
- Sharing someoneâ€™s private information without permission

## Reporting
Report incidents to: **support@kubiya.ai**
Please include: what happened, where/when, who was involved, and any screenshots/links.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take any action needed to keep the community safe, including:
- Removing or editing content
- Issuing warnings
- Temporary suspension from participation
- Permanent ban for serious or repeated violations
